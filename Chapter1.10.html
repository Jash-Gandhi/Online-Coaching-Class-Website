CHAPTER 1: Matter and its Classification

Matter refers to the material things around us which have mass, occupied space and its presence can be felt by any one or more of our five senses.
Everything in this universe is made up of material that scientists have named ‘matter’.
For example- the water, air, stars, plants, a small drop of water, or even a small sand particle is matter, but emotions like love, hate, hurt, etc. are feelings but not matter.
